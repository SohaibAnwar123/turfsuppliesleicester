Growers and suppliers of quality turf

Here at DH Goddard, we supply the highest quality lawn turf to both the public and trade. We are a family run business, established for over 60 years. Our lawn turf is a very high quality and is great for domestic gardens due to its hard-wearing properties. It is weed treated and fertilised, and delivered across Leicestershire. All of our turf is grown locally in Leicester, on our own farm by fully qualified staff. It comes in easy to manage rolls, with a laying service available if required. All our turf is cut freshly, and is prepared to order. Small orders can be collected and deliveries are sent quickly and efficiently, to give the grass the best possible chance of successfully transplanting on to your new lawn. We treat each customer as an individual, priding ourselves on the personal care and attention that you expect.
